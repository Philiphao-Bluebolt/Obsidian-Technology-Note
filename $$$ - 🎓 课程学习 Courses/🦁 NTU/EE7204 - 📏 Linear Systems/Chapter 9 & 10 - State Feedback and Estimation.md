
**Feedback** is an essential concept in control theory. They're introduced to a system, improving its performance, such as stability, accuracy, and speed, through shifting the original poles. **State feedback** generates the feedback signal based on the system's state, rather than the output. As a result, for those unobservable system, we need to design an **Estimator** to take a peep at the state.
