## Questions

> **Are globally asymptotically stable and uniformly asymptotically stable the same?**

No, "globally" refers to the state space and "uniformly" refers to the time axis. The former means that all initial states $x_0$ converge to the same equilibrium, while the latter means that the stability of the system is not affected by the time $t$
